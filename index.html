Welcome to my personal professional webpage. I am a theoretical physicist working in the fields of astrophysics and cosmology.

Since June 2013, I am a Graduate Student in Physics/Astronomy at Vanderbilt University in the group of Prof. Andreas Berlind. Before this, I spent 4 years in Florida. I am originally from Guatemala.

My research interests and methods include:

  • Statistical Analysis of synthetic Datasets
  • Clusters of Galaxies and large-scale Structure
